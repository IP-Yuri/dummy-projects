01 — The Vision

A brutalist structure, softened by the Mediterranean sun.

Designed by the acclaimed Studio AVYR, the residence is not merely a home, but a study in light and shadow.

Nestled on the cliffside, the architecture retreats into the landscape. The raw concrete walls are juxtaposed against warm travertine floors and floor-to-ceiling glass that dissolves the boundary between the interior sanctuary and the wild horizon. This is silence, engineered.
